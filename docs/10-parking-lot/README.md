# Parking lot

### Purpose

The parking lot collects ideas that are not part of the current MVP roadmap.

Ideas may include product features, operational concepts, partnerships, or strategic directions.

Ideas stored here should not influence immediate development priorities.

The purpose of this section is to preserve creative thinking without disrupting execution discipline.

Ideas may be revisited after MVP launch and early marketplace validation.

Maintaining a structured idea repository prevents valuable concepts from being forgotten.

### Future product features

Future product features may include a bidding system for high-demand slots.

Auction-based mechanics may allow users to compete for premium last-minute availability.

Providers may be able to configure dynamic pricing for slots.

Users may receive personalized slot recommendations based on past bookings.

A loyalty program may reward frequent users with platform benefits.

Future product features should only be implemented after validating the core booking model.
